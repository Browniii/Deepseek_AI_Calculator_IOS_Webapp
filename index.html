Info & App Guides

Installing the iOS WebApp

This application is precisely engineered for iPhone screens. To get the seamless, full-screen experience:

  • Open this link in Safari.
  • Tap the Share icon at the bottom center.
  • Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Launch the app directly from your home screen!

Getting a Free Gemini API Key

Gemini provides state-of-the-art vision models for free. To unlock camera features:

  • Navigate to Google AI Studio.
  • Sign in with your Google Account.
  • Click Create API Key.
  • Copy the text and paste it into the Gemini API Key box in settings.

Interacting with the AI

Tap the to enter AI Mode.

  • Use the AI keyboard or tap the top text area to use your system keyboard.
  • Tap = to Send/Solve.
  • Tap the (top left) to instantly return to the normal calculator.
  • Tap the (top right) to view your History Log.

Using the Camera

  • In AI Mode, tap 0 to open the lens, then 0 again to snap.
  • Tap - to retake, . to cancel, and = to solve!
  • Tap +/- during camera mode to hot-swap engines (Gemini vs DeepSeek).
